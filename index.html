ZeroNRW AI

ZeroNRW AI is designed to reduce Non-Revenue Water (NRW) in industries, housing societies, commercial spaces, and utilities.

  • Step 1: Smart Bhujal + You
  • Step 2: We deploy a RL AI agent to detect and minimize water losses.
  • Step 3: We provide your baseline usages and powerful analytics.
  • Step 4: Then we estimate your water savings so you can positively claim your impact
  • Step 5: Convert your water savings to carbon savings to double your impact
  • Step 6: Meet your water targets, save yourself and the Earth from water risk!

With ZeroNRW AI, you can ensure optimal water usage, reduce operational costs, and contribute to sustainable water management practices.

GWFlow AI (Under Development)

GWFlow AI allows remote groundwater level estimation without a pumping test, aiding real-estate builders, house buyers, and commercial spaces.

  • Step 1: Smart Bhujal + You
  • Step 2: You will share the coordinates to estimate groundwater levels below your building
  • Step 3: We will give you a AI-driven groundwater level range (±5m)

With GWFlow AI:

  • Real-estate builders can save significant costs without the need for a pumping test.
  • Flat buyers can make quicker decisions without consulting other flat owners.
  • Commercial spaces can showcase their groundwater level improvements, enhancing sustainability ratings.
